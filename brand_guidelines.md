## 1. Brand Core & Identity

BazaarBee is more than a grocery delivery service; it is a trusted household partner for families in Rangpur. Our brand identity balances local warmth, absolute dependability, and digital simplicity.

### Vision
> Become the most trusted monthly grocery shopping platform in Bangladesh.

### Mission
Provide every household with genuine daily essentials at fair prices through a simple digital shopping experience and dependable scheduled delivery.

### Brand Personality Attributes
* **Dependable (ভরসাযোগ্য):** We deliver original goods, on time, with transparent pricing.
* **Warm & Welcoming (আন্তরিক):** We speak like a helpful neighbor, using familiar and friendly local tones.
* **Efficient (স্মার্ট ও সহজ):** We eliminate clutter, making ordering and receiving goods effortless.
* **Local First (রংপুরের আপন):** We understand and celebrate the unique community and lifestyle of Rangpur.

## 2. Logo System

The BazaarBee logo represents our dual focus: a friendly bee (speed, service) and a market basket (groceries, abundance).

### Primary Logo
The primary logo consists of the **Mascot Icon** combined with the **Wordmark**.
* **Visual Icon:** A stylized, friendly honeybee in flight, holding a small market basket.
* **Wordmark Typography:** Set in **Outfit Bold**, with rounded, friendly geometric curves. "Bazaar" is colored in Charcoal Grey (`#1F2937`), and "Bee" is in Honey Gold (`#F2B705`) with a subtle stinger-like curve in the terminal of the 'e'.

### App Icon / Monogram
For small formats (e.g., browser favicon, mobile app icon, social media avatar), use the bee's face centered inside a warm gold hexagon (resembling a honeycomb cell).

### Clear Space
* Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to at least **1.5x the width of the bee's wings** (denoted as $X$) to prevent visual crowding.

### Logo Misuse (What NOT to do)
* **DO NOT** stretch or distort the proportions of the logo.
* **DO NOT** change the approved colors.
* **DO NOT** place the logo on busy, high-contrast photographic backgrounds without a semi-transparent container card.
* **DO NOT** rotate the bee at unnatural angles (keep its flight angle between $5^{\circ}$ and $15^{\circ}$ upwards).

## 3. Mascot Rules: "Buzz" (বাজ)

Our mascot, **Buzz** (known locally as **Bee-bhai** or **মধুভাই**), represents the service-oriented, hardworking spirit of BazaarBee.

> [!NOTE]
> Buzz is not just a decoration; he is a UI guide, customer support agent, and brand ambassador who helps users navigate their monthly grocery lists.

### Visual Rules
1. **Attire:** Buzz always wears a small, dark grey delivery cap with a gold bee-wing emblem.
2. **Expressions:** Buzz should look happy, eager to help, and reassuring. He must never look angry, aggressive, or overly hyperactive.
3. **Props:** Buzz can carry a grocery basket, a cardboard delivery box, a checklist, or a mobile phone showing savings.

### Do's & Don't's
* **DO** use Buzz to highlight savings, guide users during checkout, or celebrate a placed order.
* **DO** use clean, flat-vector illustrations with subtle gradients.
* **DON'T** use 3D renders or highly detailed drawings of bees.
* **DON'T** place Buzz in scenarios unrelated to grocery shopping or delivery.

## 4. Color Palette

Our colors reflect the warmth of honey, the freshness of groceries, and the trust of a professional delivery service.

| Color Role | Color Name | HEX Code | HSL Value | Primary Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary** | Honey Gold | `#F2B705` | `HSL(45, 96%, 48%)` | Primary brand identity, buttons, links, highlights |
| **Secondary Accent** | Honey Amber | `#D97706` | `HSL(33, 91%, 44%)` | Hover states, secondary buttons, warning alerts |
| **Fresh Accent** | Fresh Mint | `#10B981` | `HSL(160, 84%, 39%)` | Savings indicators, discount badges, success states |
| **Deep Neutral** | Charcoal Grey | `#1F2937` | `HSL(215, 28%, 17%)` | Headings, body text, primary interface structure |
| **Soft Neutral** | Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | `HSL(0, 0%, 100%)` | Card backgrounds, page layouts |
| **Warm Neutral** | Cream Honey | `#FFFBEB` | `HSL(48, 100%, 96%)` | Page background, cards, glassmorphic backing |
| **Muted Neutral** | Border Grey | `#E5E7EB` | `HSL(220, 13%, 91%)` | Borders, dividers, disabled states |

### UI Color Applications
* **Buttons:** Honey Gold (`#F2B705`) with Charcoal text (`#1F2937`) for high legibility.
* **Hover State:** Honey Amber (`#D97706`).
* **Savings/Discounts:** Fresh Mint (`#10B981`) text or badge background to immediately call out customer savings.
* **Backgrounds:** A soft transition from Clean White to Cream Honey (`#FFFBEB`) to create a warm, premium feel.

## 5. Typography

We use modern, highly readable web typography.

### Heading Font: **Outfit**
* **Provider:** Google Fonts
* **Style:** Geometric Sans-Serif with friendly, slightly rounded endings.
* **Usage:** All main page headers, section titles, and marketing slogans.
* **Weights:** Bold (700) for H1/H2, Medium (500) for H3.

### Body & UI Font: **Inter**
* **Provider:** Google Fonts
* **Style:** Highly readable, neutral sans-serif designed for user interfaces.
* **Usage:** Body copy, labels, inputs, navigation links, and descriptions.
* **Weights:** Regular (400) for general text, Semi-Bold (600) for UI controls.

### Type Scale (Desktop & Mobile)

```
Title 1 (Hero)     -> 36px / Line Height: 1.2  (Outfit Bold)
Heading 1 (Page)   -> 28px / Line Height: 1.3  (Outfit Bold)
Heading 2 (Card)   -> 20px / Line Height: 1.4  (Outfit Medium)
Body Regular       -> 16px / Line Height: 1.6  (Inter Regular)
UI Label / Small   -> 14px / Line Height: 1.4  (Inter Semi-Bold)
Caption / Micro    -> 12px / Line Height: 1.4  (Inter Regular)
```

## 6. Iconography Style

Icons should look modern, friendly, and consistent.

* **Line Weight:** Consistent **2px outline stroke**.
* **Corners:** Slightly rounded (minimum 4px corner radius) to match the friendly geometric curves of the brand.
* **Coloring:** Outline in Charcoal Grey (`#1F2937`), with a spot-fill of Honey Gold (`#F2B705`) for active states or visual interest.
* **Categories:** 
  * *Shopping:* Basket, Cart, Product box, Tag.
  * *Logistics:* Delivery truck, Clock, Map pin.
  * *Trust:* Verified checkmark, Shield, Authentic seal.

## 7. Tone of Voice & Writing Style

Our voice is **neighborly, transparent, helpful, and optimistic**. We avoid formal corporate speak and overly complicated jargon.

### Language Approach (Bilingual / 'Banglish' Context)
For local markets, we communicate primarily in standard Bengali, using simple English terms for digital functions (e.g., "Checkout", "Order") that are widely understood.

### Tone Pillars
1. **Clear & Direct:** No hidden costs, no confusing terms. We communicate delivery times and savings clearly.
2. **Respectful & Caring:** Treat every customer like family. If a delivery is delayed, apologize sincerely and offer solutions.
3. **Encouraging Savings:** Highlight how much they saved on their monthly list.

### Sample Messaging Examples

| Situation | Tone (Avoid) | BazaarBee Way (Approved) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Welcome Screen** | "Please log in to continue." | "স্বাগতম! আপনার মাসিক বাজার সহজ ও সাশ্রয়ী করতে লগইন করুন।" *(Welcome! Log in to make your monthly groceries easy and affordable.)* |
| **Delivery Selection** | "Choose logistics slot." | "আপনার সুবিধাজনক ডেলিভারি সময়টি বেছে নিন:" *(Choose your convenient delivery slot:)* |
| **Order Success** | "Order #1024 confirmed." | "অর্ডার নিশ্চিত হয়েছে! বাজ ভাই সঠিক সময়ে বাজার পৌঁছে দিতে প্রস্তুত!" *(Order confirmed! Buzz-bhai is ready to deliver on time!)* |

## 8. Brand Applications & UI Layout Guidelines

To ensure the brand remains premium, all digital designs must leverage the brand elements:

1. **Card Layouts:** Use thin borders (`1px solid #E5E7EB`) and soft shadows (`box-shadow: 0 4px 6px -1px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`).
2. **Glassmorphism:** For interactive overlays, use backdrops with `backdrop-filter: blur(8px)` and white background color with 80% opacity (`rgba(255,255,255,0.8)`).
3. **Buttons:** Primary buttons should be colored in Honey Gold (`#F2B705`) with Charcoal text, featuring a subtle hover animation (scale up slightly, change background to `#D97706`).
4. **Mascot Integration:** Place a small greeting mascot ("Buzz") near the header or greeting block.
